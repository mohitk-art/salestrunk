What is SalesTrunk?

Short video sales courses, on demand or customized, in bite-size modules that fit into the workday. Plus online sales coaching and customized sales management training courses.

Our sales training experts design short video courses, online sales coaching and custom sales management training modules using proven e-learning theory. This powerful combination gives you something other video sales courses won’t: Lasting success through a more knowledgeable sales team.

Why SalesTrunk?

Quality

E-learning professionals understand how students absorb and retain knowledge using online content, which is different from classroom learning styles. Sales experts armed with this understanding design our courses in manageable “bites” that can more easily be retained.
